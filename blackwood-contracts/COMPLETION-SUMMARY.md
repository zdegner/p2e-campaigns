### Story Coherence ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**✅ Mystery Structure:**
- Clear inciting incident (Rivermeet massacre)
- Progressive clue revelation across 30 sessions
- Multiple discovery paths (prevents dead-ends)
- Escalating stakes (monsters → conspiracy → apocalypse)
- Satisfying resolutions at each act
- Multiple ending options (player choice matters)

**✅ Witcher-Style Monster Hunting:**
- Investigation-first approach (gather intel before fighting)
- Research mechanics (identify weaknesses, craft specialized gear)

## Key Strengths

### 1. Mystery-Driven Structure ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Every session advances the investigation. Clues build organically. Players feel like detectives, not just monster-slayers.

### 2. Witcher-Style Gameplay ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Research before fighting. Preparation reduces difficulty. Contracts are varied. Monsters have weaknesses to exploit.

### 3. Moral Complexity ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Venn is tragic, not pure evil. Some monsters are victims. Corruption offers power at cost. Multiple endings respect choices.

### 4. Living World Consequences ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Ignore threats = consequences. Factions act independently. Doom clocks create urgency. NPCs don't wait for heroes.

### 5. GM Support Tools ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Cheatsheet keeps everything accessible. Templates maintain consistency. Pacing knobs adjust difficulty. Quick-start gets GM running tonight.
